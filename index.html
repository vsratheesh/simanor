About us

simanor provides M&A and other strategic advisory service to middle-market companies located in US and India. Our team is dedicated to identifying opportunities and successfully executing transaction in these market. Simanor Principals have over 150 year of combined experience in M&A, corporate development, strategic planning, operation, finance & technology.

Our Services

Buy-Side Engagements

  • Review client's growth objective & desktop acquisition criteria.
  • Identify potential target companies that meets criteria and assist client in prioritizing target to persue.
  • Provides preliminary valuation and deal structure options.

Sell-side Engagements
